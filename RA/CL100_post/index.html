Back in early November users were given a survey about who they thought was important to RA. We took that to events team, we got some input from Scott as well, and we widdled the list down. It took weeks to really shape the list and it easily could have had 50 more users. We tried to have a list of current and historical users that contributed.

Our criteria was contribution
-Development
-Moderation
-Administration
-Art
-Writing/Editing
-Community and Social Prescense

So each person on this list is represented with a gamer challenge and a developer challenge option for those wanting to mix and match. The Event will start 1/1/2023 with 10 challenges available to take on at your own pace during 2023.
